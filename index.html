About Yako

Yako Treats is a Small Online Based F&B Business focused on selling Desserts as its main product and was established in August 2020 with Panna Cotta as its first product. However, we are temporarily close in February 2021.
After going through various considerations, finally we reopen in July 2022 and introduce Fudgy Brownies as our main product.


How to Order?

Orders will be ready every Monday and made with a Pre-Order system via WhatsApp or Instagram.
Free delivery fee for Sungai Raya, Southeast Pontianak and South Pontianak areas.
